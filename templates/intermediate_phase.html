Curriculum Focus

This phase moves beyond basics, focusing on fluency, structured writing, and effective real-world communication.

  • **Skills Development:** Essay writing, report structure, debate, and presentation skills.
  • **Cultural Context:** Understanding English in various social and professional settings.
